The Five heavenly crowns awarded on Judgement Day:

  • 1) THE IMPERISHABLE CROWN - (1 Corinthians 9:25) Reserved for those who do not store up treasures on earth.

    You should not be tempted by greed, coveting, or materialism.

    For those who combat fleshly lusts, resist pampering the body, practicing the exercises of hardship, pursuing the interest of the soul, combatting fleshly lusts, combatting the appetite).

  • 2) THE CROWN OF REJOICING - (Soul winners crown)

    The Soul Winners crown : A person who rejoices to winning someone to Jesus Christ (1 Thess 2:19).

    REJOICE THAT WE HAVE CHRISTIANITY to form the Body of Christ, BECAUSE GOD WILL REMOVE DEATH, SORROW, and CRYING.

    The Soul winner spends their time Spreading the word and Saving Souls, and Rejoicing in Christianity.

  • 3) RIGHTEOUSNESS - (2 Timothy 4:8) - Crown of Righteousness

    Loving and Longing for his coming - trusing him as the Right and Just Judge, for THOSE WHO ENDURE DISCOURAGEMENTS, PERSECUTIONS, SUFFERINGS OR DEATH, FOR CHRIST, WITH NON-WAVERING LOVE FOR THE LORD deserve the Crown of Righteousness.

    You rebuke other false religions: Hinduism, Islam, Roman Catholicism and maintain your faith of Christianity even through persecution.

    Maintaining your loyalty to Jesus & the Christian Religion.

    Going the extra mile: A lukewarm Christian would not earn this crown, but a Christian who would be called "goody two shoes" on earth would receive this reward for going the extra mile - one who will always make the right decision when tempted.

  • 4) CROWN OF GLORY: (Teachers Crown/ Crown for the Shepherd) (1 Peter 5:1-4)

    Awarded for Feeding the Sheep, spreading the word, And Teaching the Word.

    Consider that we pay a small price here on earth to receive Heaven in its infinite Glory.

    For I consider that the sufferings of this present time are not worthy to be compared with the glory which shall be revealed in us. (Romans 8:18)

    The Crown Of Glory is reserved for those who took care of Jesus' sheep: (Peter). Spreading the word and Teaching the Word (Feeding the Sheep) may allow you to earn a Crown of Glory.

    Shepherds lead the flock to God, while teachers teach the word.

  • 5) CROWN OF LIFE: (James 1:12) - (The Martyrs crown)

    Resist temptation, fight the flesh, don't give in to Temptation.

    For those who endure severe hardship, testing, and tribulation.

    For those who endured sufferings (such as being thrown in Prison for him), who bravely confront persecution, who receive Jesus as the Bread of Life.
